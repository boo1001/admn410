History

We are Durham House of Pets, the number #1 Pet Supply store in New England! Founded in 2014 by Ben O'Connell, the brand has grown from 1 small shop in Durham,to 20 stores around the east coast! In our stores you will find a comforting enviorment for all your pet needs!

When Ben had is first store in 2014 they were so poor that they only sold Dog and Cat food. Since then Ben has grown the brand into a store that sells over 250 different brands and supplies for over 70 different animals! With plans evolving everyday we can't wait for what the future has for Ben and our company!

Why Use Us?

Our stores offer the most bang for your buck out of all other pet stores. We sell everything from Dog food, Pet enclosures, to live animals! When you come to our store we will make sure you leave satisfied and content with your trip!

Our staff go through a 2 week training course designed to teach the ins and outs of the pet industry, with experts from snakes to hamsters you will find everything you need in our store!
